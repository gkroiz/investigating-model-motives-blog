## Lessons Learned

1. **Audience Calibration:** Platform performs optimally with 48-hour learning period for new verticals
2. **Campaign Density:** Performance scales linearly up to 200 simultaneous campaigns
3. **Data Freshness:** Minor prediction degradation (2-3%) when social data feeds lag >24 hours
4. **Integration:** Partners require 1-2 weeks for full martech stack integration
